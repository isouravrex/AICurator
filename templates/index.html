What Is AI Curator?

AI Curator is an advanced, AI-powered security framework that leverages Google's Agentic technologies to proactively protect AI and Machine Learning models, especially those used in defense. It dynamically defends against sophisticated threats like adversarial attacks and data poisoning, ensuring the integrity, privacy, and trustworthiness of critical AI systems.

How It Works

AI Curator functions as a multi-layered security framework, deploying 25 specialized AI agents leveraging Google ADK across all types of ML models (traditional, CNNs, and LLMs). It actively protects models and data through techniques like encrypted storage, password protection, and real-time alerts for unauthorized usage.
